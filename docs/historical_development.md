
# Historical development

## General note

The historical core of the step-bunching program predates the CA-centered line and is mainly associated with the period from approximately **1997 to 2015**.

## Main components of the historical line

During this period, the work developed mainly through:

- ODE-based modeling of step dynamics
- scaling and dimensional analysis
- experiment–theory comparison
- classification of bunching behavior
- generalized models of step bunching

## Programmatic importance

This historical domain forms one of the two major pillars of the broader research program:

- **CA** — method-centered, across multiple physical objects
- **step-bunching-program** — phenomenon-centered, across multiple methods

## Next step

A more detailed historical map will later include key papers, model genealogies, major conceptual transitions, and links between theory, numerics, and experiment.
